Mission Statement

Ultimate Auto Sales was founded in the Summer of 2011. Our mission is provide high quality, used automobiles to our clients at a fair and affordable price. As opposed to other car dealerships, we are a whole sale, independent dealership that focuses on finding the best match that fits our client's needs. Our clients may select a car from our current inventory or provide the details of their dream car to Mr. Assaf, and he will find the right car for them.

About Us

In the mid 90's, Ehab and Laila Assaf moved to the United States, all the way from Egypt, with the American Dream. With plenty of experience in sales and hospitality, and an unwavering passion for cars, the Assaf family opened Ultimate Auto Sales in 2011. We are a 100% family owned, small business based out of Greensboro, NC. Our family has lived in Greensboro for over 20 years and have always loved the piedmont triad area. Since our founding, we have focused on delivering high quality, reliable used cars to our fellow North Carolinians at an honest and fair price. The owner, Ehab Assaf is true family man and a car fanatic. As a teenager he worked hard to purchase his first car, a bright red 1972 Alfa Romeo. From that moment, Ehab found his passion in automobiles and the auto industry. Today, Ehab is the father of 2 young men, Abdurrahman and Abdullah, and the husband of his lovely wife, Laila. The Assaf family and Ultimate Auto Sales still call Greensboro their home, til this day.

Disclaimer to the Client

Ultimate Auto Sales does not finance automobiles to clients. All automobiles must be paid in full in order to receive the title. All financing inquiries can go through banks, third party financing companies, or other legal sources of loans. We appreciate your cooperation and business.
